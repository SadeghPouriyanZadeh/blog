HMIB (Hierarchical Modular Intelligent Being)

today i dreamed about an idea that what if there would be an intelligent being that is consisted of multiple intelligent beings that are related to each other in a hierarchical manner. they can interact with each other. the higher-level intelligent being can override the lower-level one whenever it wants. the lower-level is completely independent unless the higher-level one overrides it.
